# Skill: Automation Funnel Design

## Purpose
Design high-conversion automated funnel systems that scale revenue sustainably and maximize ROI, moving beyond superficial impression metrics.

## Core Principles
1. **Focus on Outcomes**: Prioritize lead quality and revenue impact over vanity metrics like impressions.
2. **Scalability**: Build systems that can handle increased traffic without manual intervention.
3. **Sustainable Growth**: Ensure every step of the funnel is optimized for long-term ROI rather than quick fixes.

## Implementation Strategy
- **Lead Generation**: Align advertising strategies with the intended funnel entry point.
- **Automation Workflow**: Utilize tools to nurture leads automatically after they enter the funnel.
- **Data-Driven Optimization**: 
    - Always evaluate performance against targets (CPA/ROAS).
    - Use the `analyze_roas_cpa` tool to identify bottlenecks in the funnel.

## Instruction for AI Agents
When tasked with designing or auditing a funnel:
1. Analyze the current traffic sources and ad strategies.
2. Assess if the current funnel supports sustainable revenue growth.
3. Propose automation improvements to increase ROI while keeping CPA within the defined `EDUCATION_KPI` threshold.
